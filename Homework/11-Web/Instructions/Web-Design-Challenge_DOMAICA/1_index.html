Summary: Latitude vs. X


In this project we have tried to verify how latitude (Locations situated farther or closer to the equator) influences or affects meteorological variables such as wind, temperature, humidity or cloudiness.

The sample on which the statistical study has been carried out has been obtained from OpenWeatherMap.com and contains meteorological data from 547 randomly selected cities and towns around the world.

  • Data is collected in .csv format

  • Graphics have been made with Matplotlib

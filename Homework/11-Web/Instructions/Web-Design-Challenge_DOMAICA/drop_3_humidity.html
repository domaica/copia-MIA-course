Cities latitude vs Humidity

With the sample data we can conclude that there is no observable correlation between latitude and the degree of humidity of the environment.

There are populations with 100% humidity in any part of the globe regardless of the position with respect to the equator. The data are completely heterogeneous and diverse.

As an exception we can see that there are populations between latitude 60 and 75 degrees in the northern hemisphere with 0% humidity. may be due to sample or may be outliers
